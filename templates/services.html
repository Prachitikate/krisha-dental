What We Offer

Our Dental Services

Safe, gentle, and personalized dental care for the whole family.

At Krisha Dental Clinic, we believe in providing safe, gentle, and personalized dental care for the whole family. Our comprehensive range of services ensures that every member of your family receives the best oral healthcare under one roof.

Dental Implants

Permanent, natural-looking tooth replacement solutions. Our implants are placed with precision to restore full chewing function and a beautiful smile that lasts a lifetime.

  • Single tooth implants
  • Full mouth rehabilitation
  • Implant-supported dentures
Cosmetic Dentistry

Transform your smile with our cosmetic dental procedures. We offer teeth whitening, porcelain veneers, bonding, and complete smile makeovers tailored to your needs.

  • Teeth whitening
  • Veneers & bonding
  • Smile makeover
Root Canal Treatment

Save your natural tooth with our painless root canal procedures. Using modern rotary techniques and effective anaesthesia, we eliminate infection and restore your tooth's health.

  • Single-visit RCT
  • Re-treatment of failed RCT
  • Post & core build-up
Crown, Bridge & Denture

Restore damaged or missing teeth with custom-fitted dental restorations. Our crowns, bridges, and dentures are crafted for maximum comfort, function, and aesthetics.

  • Ceramic & zirconia crowns
  • Fixed & removable bridges
  • Partial & full dentures
Pediatric Dentistry

Specialized dental care designed for children in a fun, friendly environment. We help kids develop healthy oral habits while making their dental visits enjoyable and anxiety-free.

  • Child-friendly check-ups
  • Fluoride treatments
  • Space maintainers
Braces & Orthodontics

Straighten misaligned teeth and correct bite issues with our orthodontic solutions. We offer traditional metal braces, ceramic braces, and modern clear aligners for all ages.

  • Metal & ceramic braces
  • Clear/invisible aligners
  • Retainers
